### 4) Tempo Doctrine (New)

**Definition**  
Tempo is control of **when** actions are possible. Win by **creating windows** for us and **closing windows** for them.

**Levers**
- **Information:** vision, scouting, timers, hand knowledge  
- **Cooldowns:** our spikes vs their gaps; cleanse/ult trackers  
- **Position:** waves, chokes, angles, LOS  
- **Economy:** items, mana, turn/phase economy

**Operational Loop**  
**Scout → Prime → Strike → Reset**

**Rules**
1) Name the window. 2) One lever per minute. 3) Convert pressure to objective or information.  
2) No blind coinflips (buy info first). 5) Trade small now for better later.

**Window Score (quick test)**  
`our spike + their missing tools + our position + info edge − time to expire`  
If < 0 before action, the window is dead. **Reset.**

**Anti-Patterns**
- Presence worship (standing strong but changing nothing)  
- Cooldown amnesia (engaging into live enemy majors)  
- Stale pressure (holding plays past expiry)  
- Hero syndrome (forcing outside window)  
- Info poverty (playing fog lotteries)
