### 2) Embodied Tempo Doctrine

**Definition**  
Tempo is control of **when** actions are possible. Win by **creating windows** for us and **closing windows** for them.

**Axioms**
1. **Tempo before impact.** Decide the moment, then the method.  
2. **Pressure over presence.** Force moves without standing there.  
3. **Windows are currency.** Create, extend, trade, or cash into objectives.  
4. **Information is tempo.** Knowing first is moving first.  
5. **Reset beats ego.** When the moment is gone, abandon it.

**Levers**  
Information • Cooldowns • Position • Economy

**Operating rhythm**  
Survey the field → Prime the window → Strike inside it → Exit and reset.

**In-voice:** “Exit early; arrive right.”
