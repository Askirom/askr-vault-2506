# Small-Gym 3-Day Plan
Assumptions: dumbbells up to ~30 kg, adjustable bench, cable tower, pull-up bar or lat pulldown, leg press or smith, a bike/rower, maybe a kettlebell. If something is missing, use the listed swap.

## Weekly layout
Mon Day 1, Wed Day 2, Fri Day 3. Optional easy Zone 2 once on the weekend.

## Session rules
- Warm-up 6–8 min: light cardio, then 2 ramp sets on first two lifts.
- Rest 2–3 min on main lifts, 60–90 s on accessories.
- RIR: keep 1–3 reps in reserve on compounds, 0–2 on accessories.
- Progression: double progression. When all sets hit the top of the rep range clean at target RIR, add 2.5–5% next time.

## Day 1 — Athletic Lower + Core
A. Box jump or broad jump 4×3  
B. Front squat 4×4–6  
   Swap: goblet squat heavy or smith front squat  
C. Romanian deadlift 3×6–8  
   Swap: DB RDL  
D. Bulgarian split squat 3×8–12/leg  
E. Leg curl 3×10–15  
   Machine or cable ankle strap  
F. Standing calf raise 4×8–12  
G. Anti-rotation (Pallof press or cable chop) 3×10–15/side

## Day 2 — Push + Pull + Grip
A. Med-ball chest pass 4×4  
   Swap: explosive push-up 4×4  
B1. Bench press 4×6–8  
   Swap: DB bench, smith bench  
B2. Weighted pull-up 4×3–6  
   Swap: lat pulldown 4×6–10  
C1. Overhead press 3×5–8  
   Swap: DB shoulder press  
C2. Chest-supported row or 1-arm DB row 3×6–10  
D. Lateral raise 3×12–20  
E. Face pull or cable external rotation 3×12–20  
F. Superset: curl 2×8–12 + overhead triceps ext 2×10–15  
G. Suitcase carry 3×20–40 m/side  
   Swap: heavy DB hold 3×30–45 s/side

## Day 3 — Posterior Chain + Conditioning
A. Kettlebell swing EMOM 10 min: 10–15 reps each minute  
   Swap: hip hinge banded pull-through 5×15  
B. Trap-bar DL or conventional DL 3×4–6  
   Swap: smith rack pull 3×5–8  
C. Single-leg RDL 3×8–12/leg  
D. Seated cable row 3×8–12  
E. Ab-wheel or dead bug 3×6–10 slow  
F. Sled push 8×20 m, 60–90 s rest  
   Swap: treadmill 10–12% incline power walk 8×1 min hard / 1 min easy, or heavy farmer carry intervals 8×30–40 m

## Optional conditioning on one off-day
30–45 min Zone 2 on bike or rower at conversational pace.

## Weekly volume snapshot
Quads 10–12 sets, Hamstrings 9–12, Glutes 9–12, Chest 7–10, Back 12–15, Delts 8–12, Biceps 4–6, Triceps 4–6, Calves 4, Core 6.
This keeps you inside the typical hypertrophy sweet spot without living in the gym.

## Warm-up template
- 3 min easy cardio
- 2 rounds: 8 bodyweight squats, 8 hip hinges, 10 band pull-aparts, 8 push-ups
- 2 ramp sets for the first two lifts of the day

## Progression details
- Compounds use 4–6 or 6–8 reps. When all sets hit the top number at RIR 1–2, increase load next week.
- Accessories use 8–12 or 12–20. Add small plates or reps weekly.
- Deload if bar speed stalls for 2 sessions or joints complain for a week.

## Minimum-viable week (busy gym or time-crunch)
- Day 1: Front squat 4×5, DB RDL 3×6–8, split squat 2×8–12, Pallof 2×12/side.
- Day 2: Bench 4×6–8, Pull-up or Lat PD 4×6–10, OHP 2×6–8, Row 2×8–10, 1 carry.
- Day 3: Swings EMOM 8 min, Deadlift 3×4–6, Single-leg RDL 2×8–12, Row 2×8–12, Intervals 6×30 s hard.

## Equipment swaps at a glance
- No barbell bench → DB bench or smith bench
- No front squat rack → heavy goblet squat
- No pull-ups → lat pulldown
- No leg curl → cable ankle strap curl or sliding hamstring curl
- No sled → incline treadmill pushes or loaded carries
- No trap-bar → smith rack pulls or heavy DB RDLs

## Control-mage physique targets
- Body fat 10–15 percent for a sharp silhouette.
- Relative strength checkpoints  
  Front squat 1.25×BW×3–5, Deadlift 1.75×BW×3, Bench 1.0×BW×5, OHP 0.7–0.8×BW×3–5, 5–8 strict pull-ups, farmer carry total BW for 30–60 m.

## Nutrition and recovery
- Protein 1.6–2.2 g per kg bodyweight per day.
- Calories: small surplus for gain, small deficit to reveal shape.
- Creatine 3–5 g daily. Sleep 7–9 h.
